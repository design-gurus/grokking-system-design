# Roadmaps

Pick the plan that fits your timeline.

| Plan | Best when | Time per day |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| [1-week crash plan](1-week-plan.md) | Your interview is days away | 2 to 3 hours |
| [2-week sprint](2-week-plan.md) | You have a couple of weeks | 1 to 2 hours |
| [6-week study plan](6-week-plan.md) | You are building depth from a baseline | About 1 hour |

Whatever the timeline, the order is the same: learn the [framework](../cheat-sheets/interview-framework.md) and [patterns](../patterns/) first, then practice with [questions](../questions/), then do timed [mock interviews](https://www.designgurus.io/mock-interviews).
